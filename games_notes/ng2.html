Ninja Gaiden 2

The goal of my first run is to end the game at normal difficulty, using ONLY the Dragon Sword and collecting all the skulls. Here there is a list of chapters, to keep track: -
  • Chapter 1: In the City, trying to save Sonia who knows about great fiend. But it was only a method to distract me while they attack the Hayabusa Village.
  • Chapter 2: I go back to my village, in flame, and at the end help my father in fighting the enemy ninja. Me + father VS Enemy + Woman. I follow the woman, who escapes with the demon statue. My father stays in the village, fighing the Enemy.
  • Chapter 3: Because of the Demon Statue, dark force is unleashed on Manhattan. I go there and I find my way between destroyed buildings, ultimately going in the underground where I kill the electric worm. But the thunder power of the Fiends is still active.
  • Chapter 4: The goal is to fight the "main Fiend of thunder", who controls lightinings from the peak of the Statue of Liberty. Then I emerge from the underground and find my way until facing him.
And here summing up the save slots:
  • 1: Spider Boss, level 1
  • 2: Spider Boss again, level 2
  • 3: Ending boss of chapter 2, the flaming ninja
  • 4: Ending boss of chapter 3, the electric worm
  • 5: miniboss of chapter 4, dark souls
  • 6: current
